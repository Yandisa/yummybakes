Frequently Asked Questions

Here’s everything you need to know before you order.

{% comment %} 1. Delivery {% endcomment %}

Yes, we do! We deliver within Tsomo, Cofimvaba, Ngcobo, Ngqamakhwe, and surrounding areas. Delivery fees vary based on your location.
{% comment %} 2. Custom order {% endcomment %}

You can use our Order page or contact us directly via WhatsApp with your custom request.
{% comment %} 3. Payment {% endcomment %}

We currently accept EFT (Electronic Funds Transfer) and cash. Card payments will be available soon — stay tuned!
{% comment %} 4. Order timing {% endcomment %}

For custom cakes, 3–5 days’ notice is ideal. For cupcakes or cookies, 1–2 days is usually enough.
{% comment %} 5. Confirmation {% endcomment %}

Once we receive your order details, we’ll send a confirmation via WhatsApp or email. Orders are only confirmed after at least 50% payment (for EFT payments).
{% comment %} 6. Flavors {% endcomment %}

We bake classics like vanilla, chocolate, red velvet, lemon, caramel, and carrot. Custom flavors can be arranged too!
{% comment %} 7. Gifting {% endcomment %}

Definitely! Just provide their name, address, and phone number, and we’ll handle the sweet surprise.
{% comment %} 8. Cancellation {% endcomment %}

You can cancel up to 24 hours before pickup or delivery. Custom orders may not be refundable once baking has started.
{% comment %} 9. Pickup {% endcomment %}

Absolutely! Pickup is available in Tsomo. We’ll confirm the time and address once your order is placed.
{% comment %} 10. Custom Designs {% endcomment %}

Yes! We love bringing your ideas to life. Just share your theme or inspiration when placing your order.
